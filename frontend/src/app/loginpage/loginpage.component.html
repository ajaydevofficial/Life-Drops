About lifedrops

Your blood can give a life to someone!
Lifedrops helps you to search, notify and connect thousands of blood donors. A blood donor can select any specific date and location to donate blood and find a suitable person who needs blood. Connecting blood donors & needy reduces time which increases the possibility of saving lives & also eliminate the shortage of blood.

Note : All users have to sign in for using this website, this is for preventing misuse of the platform
